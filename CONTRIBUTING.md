Our goal is to build a structured, clear, and accessible Arabic-learning resource for global learners.

Please follow the rules below when contributing:

---

## 1. Contribution Types
You may contribute by:
- Improving explanations
- Fixing spelling or grammar issues (Arabic + English)
- Adding examples or practice exercises
- Suggesting better lesson structure
- Reporting mistakes through GitHub Issues

## 3. Writing Rules
### For Arabic content:
- Use clear Modern Standard Arabic  
- Avoid dialect except when teaching beginner pronunciation  
- Explain grammar in a simple way  

### For English content:
- Keep explanations simple  
- Use beginner-friendly vocabulary  
- Avoid long, complex sentences  

---

## 4. Pull Requests
Before submitting a Pull Request:
1. Review your content  
2. Check grammar/spelling  
3. Explain clearly what you changed  

PRs with unclear descriptions may be rejected.
